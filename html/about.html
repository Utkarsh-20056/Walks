Our Mission

To remove the financial and logistical barriers that prevent college students and staff from exploring the world around them. We believe travel should be accessible, affordable, and easy to plan with friends.

The Walks Story

Walks was born out of shared frustration. Founders Tanvi, Prachi, and Anjali—college classmates—spent countless hours coordinating budgets, routes, and accommodation for their own weekend trips. They realized there was no single platform designed specifically for the budget-conscious group traveler. Walks solves that problem by integrating planning, deals, and local contacts into one simple tool.
